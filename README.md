# 📊 Deloitte HR Dashboard (2018–2026)

An interactive **Power BI HR Analytics Dashboard** designed to analyze workforce performance, employee distribution, salary trends, and attrition risks across Deloitte departments. The dashboard enables HR teams and business leaders to make data-driven decisions through dynamic KPIs and visual insights.

## 🚀 Features
- 👥 Total Employees: **100K**
- 💰 Average Salary: **₹187.47K**
- 📈 Average Experience: **12 Years**
- ⭐ Average Rating: **4/5**
- 📊 Department-wise Employee Distribution
- 💵 Average Monthly Salary by Grade
- 🔄 Attrition Risk Analysis
- 📈 Total Monthly Salary by Department
- 🎯 Interactive Slicers for Hire Date, Department, and Attrition Risk

## 📌 Key Insights
- Consulting department has the highest number of employees (**14.5K**).
- M1 and M2 grades receive the highest average salary (**₹188K**).
- Low attrition risk employees account for approximately **33.44%** of the workforce.
- Consulting department generates the highest monthly salary expenditure (**₹2.726M**).
